✦ Under the Hood

How the AI
Diagnoses Disease

Our Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline ensures every answer is grounded in verified agricultural science.

📝
Step 1
Query Embedding
Your symptom description is converted into a high-dimensional vector embedding using Google's text embedding model, capturing semantic meaning rather than just keywords.
🔍
Step 2
Vector Search
ChromaDB performs cosine similarity search across all 427 disease vectors, retrieving the top 5 most semantically similar disease records in milliseconds.
🧠
Step 3
Gemini Analysis
Google Gemini 2.0 Flash receives both your query and the retrieved disease context. It reasons across this evidence to produce a structured diagnosis with treatment recommendations.
🌾
Step 4
Bilingual Output
The diagnosis is formatted into structured sections — disease name, symptoms, treatment, safety warnings, and biological control — all presented in both English and Urdu.
✦ Data Provenance

Verified Data

All disease records are sourced from the Pakistan Agricultural Research Council (PARC) — the country's premier agricultural research institution established in 1981.

PARC's disease database represents decades of field research across Pakistan's diverse agro-climatic zones, ensuring recommendations are locally relevant and scientifically verified.

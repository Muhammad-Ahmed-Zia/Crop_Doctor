✦ Under the Hood

How the AI Diagnoses Disease

Our RAG pipeline ensures every diagnosis is grounded in verified PARC agricultural science — not AI hallucination.

📝
Step 1
Query Embedding
Your symptom description is converted to a high-dimensional vector capturing semantic meaning — not just keywords.
🔍
Step 2
Vector Search
ChromaDB performs cosine similarity search across all 671 disease vectors, retrieving the top 5 most semantically similar records in milliseconds.
🧠
Step 3
Gemini Analysis
Google Gemini 2.0 Flash receives your query and the retrieved disease context, reasoning across evidence to produce a structured bilingual diagnosis.
🌾
Step 4
Bilingual Structured Output
Disease name, symptoms, treatment, Pakistan brand, safety warnings, and biological control — all in English and Urdu.
✦ Data Provenance

PARC Pakistan Verified Data

All disease records are sourced from the Pakistan Agricultural Research Council (PARC) — established 1981, the country's premier agricultural research institution.

PARC's disease database represents decades of field research across Pakistan's diverse agro-climatic zones, ensuring recommendations are locally relevant and scientifically verified.
